Meet the Director

Hi, My name is Emma Gardiner. I am 24 years old and just about to start my PhD at Lancaster University specialising in Virology.

I have spent the last 6 years studying at Lancaster University, where I have completed my Undergraduate degree in Biochemistry and my Masters in Biomedicine.

I specialise in sciences and can tutor these from primary school level up to masters level at university. I have been tutoring for over two years now and have learnt many different ways of tackling different students' needs and ensuring they achieve the best grade possible.
